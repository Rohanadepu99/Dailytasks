(Price: Rs. 11.32 lakh to Rs. 18.42 lakh)

2.Skoda Slavia

The Skoda Slavia is a mid-size sedan and a fully loaded car. It replaces the Skoda Rapid but is longer, feature-rich, and powerful. The interior is spacious and well-equipped and shares most of its parts with the Kushaq SUV. It shares most of its components with the newly launched Volkswagen Virtus.

(Price: Rs. 11 lakh to Rs. 18 lakh)

Specifications

Engine: 1.0-litre and 1.5-litre

Fuel type: Petrol

Gearbox: Automatic/Manual

Mileage: 15 to 18kmpl

3.Kia EV6

The Kia EV6 is a premium EV boasting 528kms of battery range and can quickly recharge in 18 mins (10 to 80%). Apart from frugal battery life, it boasts a powerful electric motor that helps it propel from 0 to 100 kmph in 5.2 seconds. The feature list is long and impressive for those looking to buy a luxury EV in India.

(Price: Rs. 60 lakh to Rs. 65 lakh)

Specifications

Motor: 77.4kWh electric motor

Fuel type: Electric

Gearbox: Automatic

Mileage: 528 kms battery range

4.Hyundai Venue

Hyundai has launched the facelift version of its sub-compact SUV, Venue. The vehicle looks more like the new-generation Hyundai Tucson and Palisade. It is an all-rounder, available in different powertrain and gearbox options; hence, it caters to various buyers. It is also a feature-packed SUV, and the engine is refined compared to some of its rivals.

(Price: Rs. 7.50 lakh to Rs. 12.50 lakh)

Specifications

Engine: 1.2 and 1.0-litre petrol and 1.5-litre diesel

Fuel type: Petrol/diesel

Gearbox: iMT and DCT (Dual Clutch Transmission)/Manual

Mileage: 19 to 20kmpl

5.Maruti Suzuki Grand Vitara

The Maruti Suzuki Grand Vitara is a mid-size hybrid SUV and the company’s first hybrid vehicle. It has a good road presence, excellent ride and handling characteristics, and modern features. The mild and strong hybrid powertrains are the biggest differentiator from its immediate rivals.

(Price: Rs. 12.22 lakh to Rs. 22.66 lakh)

Specifications

Engine: 1.5-litre

Fuel type: Petrol/Petrol-Electric hybrid

Gearbox: CVT (Continous Variable Transmission)/Manual/Torque Converter

Mileage: 20kmpl

6.Hyundai Tucson

The all-new Hyundai Tucson is the first model launched in India that boasts Hyundai’s new design language. The chrome parametric front grille with hidden Daytime Running Lights (DRLs) and LED headlights, along with the connecting LED tail lights, make the SUV sophisticated and progressive. The vehicle boasts creature comforts and advanced safety features.

(Price: Rs. 27.70 lakh to Rs. 34.55 lakh)

Specifications

Engine: 2.0-litre

Fuel type: Petrol/diesel

Gearbox: Automatic

Mileage: 15 to 18kmpl

6.Lamborghini Revuelto

The Lamborghini Revuelto has a seating capacity of 2 passengers and is priced at Rs. 8.89 crore. This ultra-sporty car is available in one variant only. Its fuel type is Hybrid and the transmission is Automatic. With an engine of 6498cc, the Lamborghini Car Price delivers 1001 PS power and can sprint from 0 to 1000 kmph in just 2.5 seconds. The features of this luxury car include advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS), lane change warning, rear cross-traffic alert, an 8.4-inch vertical infotainment display, and more. To ensure safety, this car is equipped with a total of five airbags.

(Price: Rs. 8.89 crore)

Specifications

Engine: 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 petrol engine-litre

Fuel type: Hybrid (Electric + Petrol)

Gearbox: Transverse rear gearbox
